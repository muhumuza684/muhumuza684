FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Still have a question? Email us at lekawewe@gmail.com — we reply within hours.

How long does it take to build my website? +
Most of our websites are delivered within 3–7 business days. Starter landing pages take 3 days, Business sites take 5 days, and E-Commerce stores take 7 days. Larger custom projects are scoped individually — you'll receive a clear timeline before any work begins. We have never missed a committed delivery date in our nearly 20 years of operation.
Will my website rank on Google? +
Yes. Every website we build includes full on-page SEO as standard — this covers keyword-optimised headings and page titles, meta descriptions, image alt tags, schema markup, fast page loading speeds, mobile optimisation, and Google Search Console integration. For clients wanting to accelerate their rankings further, we offer ongoing SEO service packages.
Is my website GDPR compliant? +
Absolutely. All websites built for European clients include GDPR-compliant cookie consent management, a privacy policy, secure data handling for form submissions, and best-practice data processing procedures. We've been building GDPR-compliant websites since the regulation came into force in 2018 and keep fully up to date with European data protection requirements.
Do I need to provide content and images? +
You can provide your own content and images, or we can help with both. We have access to premium stock photography libraries for all markets and our team can write professional copy tailored to your industry and audience. Simply let us know what you need when you enquire — we'll include everything in your quote with no hidden costs.
How do I pay — is it safe? +
We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Stripe (credit/debit card). Payment is structured as 50% to commence the project and 50% on delivery — before we hand over any files. All card transactions are processed through SSL-encrypted payment gateways. We issue formal invoices for all payments for your records.
What happens if I'm not happy with the design? +
We include unlimited revisions on all our packages. Before any coding begins, we present your design for approval. We won't consider a project complete until you're 100% satisfied. In the very rare case we cannot reach a satisfactory result together, we offer a full refund. Our 98% satisfaction rate across 2,500+ clients speaks for itself.
Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Yes. We build most sites with a user-friendly CMS (Content Management System) so you can update text, images, and content yourself without any technical knowledge. We provide a short video tutorial walking you through how to manage your site. If you'd prefer us to handle all updates for you, our monthly maintenance plans start from just €49/month.
Do you offer hosting and domain registration? +
Yes. We can handle your domain registration, hosting setup, SSL certificate, and professional email setup as part of your package — or we can deploy your site to your existing hosting provider. We work with all major platforms including Vercel, Netlify, SiteGround, and cPanel-based hosts. Hosting costs are separate and typically €5–20/month depending on your chosen provider and location.
Do you build websites in languages other than English? +
Yes — multilingual websites are one of our specialities. We regularly build sites in German, French, Dutch, Swedish, Spanish, Italian, and other European languages. You can provide your own translations or we can arrange professional translation services for you. Multilingual support is available on our Business and Enterprise packages.

You can also email us directly at lekawewe@gmail.com or send us a text on Zangi at +37-1315-4411. We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours, Monday to Friday. Once we've had an initial conversation, we'll send you a detailed proposal and fixed-price quote within 24 hours.
